Why Phoenix? The Investor's Problem

As investors, our money is scattered everywhere—broking apps for stocks, various platforms for direct mutual funds, and bank accounts for liquidity. Tracking your actual day-to-day net worth and true annualized returns (XIRR) across multiple apps becomes a chaotic, manual chore.

The Phoenix Portfolio Tracker eliminates the fragmentation. Built completely inside Google Sheets, it pulls real-time financial market parameters and calculations straight into a single private dashboard. No subscription locked inside a third-party app, no selling your financial data—just automated, institutional-grade mathematical clarity over your entire net worth.
